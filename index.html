Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! We offer a variety of plant-based milks and vegan/gluten-free pastry options.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking a table during busy weekend hours.

Our Story

"More than just a coffee shop, we are your second living room."

RISHI’d Café started with one thought: good coffee hits different when the food’s just as good and the space feels like home. Founded in the heart of the city, we set out to build a community hub where creatives, professionals, and friends could gather over a truly exceptional cup of coffee.

We’re not just about roasting beans — we’re about creating your everyday hangout. Every pastry is baked fresh daily, and every shot of espresso is pulled with precision, passion, and care by our expert baristas.

What We Stand For

Ethically Sourced

We partner directly with farmers to ensure fair wages and sustainable growing practices for every single bean.

Locally Roasted

Our signature blends are roasted in small batches right here in town to guarantee maximum freshness in your cup.

Community First

From hosting local musicians to showcasing neighborhood artists, we believe in giving back to the city we love.
